How To Vote???

There are 2 methods of voting

First Method: In-Person

  • If it is your first time voting, bring a state ID (such as driver's license)
  • Find the nearest polling site (preferably in your zip code area)
  • Fill out ballet as directed.

Second Method: By Mail

  • Request a mail ballot online from your state
  • Fill out the ballot as directed
  • Mail or give ballot to nearest polling state.

For more information, head to your state's website on voting or the US.GOV link by clicking learn more!
